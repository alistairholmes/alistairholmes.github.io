Ciibo Driver

Ciibo is the most comprehensive management platform created exclusively for Amazon Delivery Service Providers. This helps drivers to keep track of their scores, pay and work schedule. My responsibilities included setting up an automated app release process to quickly get feedback. I also improved the development workflow, communication between the product team, added new features, fixed bugs and finally launched the app.

  • REST API
  • Bloc
